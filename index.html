ONLINE GIVING

The truth is giving tithes and offerings is that we bring ourselves closer to Him (God) when we give. Jesus says in Matthew 6:21, “For where your treasure is, there will your heart be also.” Giving tithes and offerings puts our love for God before our worldly wealth and shows that our heart is always with Him.

OUR VISION, AND MISSION STATEMENTS

Below is our vision, mission statements

VISION

1) To make heaven.

2) To take as many people with us.

3) To have a member of RCCG in every family of all nations.

MISSION

1) To accomplish No. 1 in the vision statement, holiness will be our lifestyle.

2) To accomplish No. 2 and 3 in the vision statement, we will plant churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing countries and within five minutes driving distance in every city and town of developed countries.

3) We will pursue these objectives until every Nation in the world is reached for the Lord Jesus Christ
